Mr Sampler is getting a re-work as part of a school project for the time of the COVID-19 crisis .

Service Announcement

Most of the students responsible for the Mr. Sampler project are no longer taking classes at the Sekundarschule Kreuzau/Nidgeggen, and therefore cannot work on this website any longer. We—the Mr. Sampler team—decided to keep the website up for teachers to showcase it.

We also want to thank Ann-Marie Müller, who served proudly as Mr. Sampler’s CEO in class and on events. Without her, this project would have never been this successful.

Best of luck to all students.

Project Name: Mr Sampler  ·  Teacher: Ann-Marie Müller  ·  Unit: World of Work


A word from our CEO:

“Mr. Sampler” is a school project completed by class 9d at the “Sekundarschule Kreuzau – Nideggen”

The concept was for the class to found a production company, build a prototype in a student-designed production hall, “sell” the product, hire employees and create a website. We are also planning a company Christmas party, to which the school directors, form teachers, and the teacher responsible for their practical training were invited.

With this work, we hoped to achieve a somewhat realistic appreciation for “The World of Work”. The class not only met these expectations, they exceeded them.




As stated on our News page, those responsible for the Mr Sampler project are no longer present at the school and can therefor not continue to work on this project as they work on more important projects nowadays.
